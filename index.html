Impact investors and grant-making foundations have come to rely on the organisations that they invest in to self-report the effectiveness of dollars spent on STEM and STEAM training and career development.

Those organisations providing training and career development recognise the need for ongoing coaching and mentorship to program participants.

There is no easy way to monitor the career progress of alumni of the program and provide a scalable way for successful alumni to pay-it-forward, until now.

RoleCoin
thrives here

RoleCoins track proof-of-progress as it happens on STEAM Role, a career-development mobile app that helps students and aspiring professionals discover and receive ongoing inspiration and guidance from diverse role models.

Through STEAM Role, RoleCoin recipients follow and learn from role models with similar background, gender, ethnicity and interests. Career develop activates and pay-it-forward role model contributions on the STEAM Role mobile app are recorded as proof-of-progress.
